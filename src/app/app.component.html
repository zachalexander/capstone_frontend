Solar panel arrays are often marketed by the number of kW they can produce. This number is actually what the system would produce assuming they receive full sunlight for at least 8 hours in a day. In reality the amount of kW they produce depends largely on the sunlight your rooftop is exposed to. The following calculator is meant to take into account the sunlight in your area and provide estimates of certain panel system sizes (since you are often limited by the size of your roof) as well as their efficiencies.
